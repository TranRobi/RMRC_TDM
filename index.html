Our team:

Kókány Corporations

We are a Hungarian team consisting of four high school students and our mentor, Mr. András Bakti. This is our second time competing, and we are very excited to put our robot, nicknamed Kókánybot, to the test.

Mester Máté

Role: Hardware

Máté focuses on the mechanical aspect of our robot, he works on the assembly of the robot. He also deals with the logistical issues for the team.

Tran Duy Dat

Role: Hardware

Dat also focuses on the mechanical aspect of the robot, he designs parts using CAD software and helps with the assembly of parts. He designed our custom robotic arm which uses stepper motors.

Vadász Bence

Role: Hardware

Bence is the newest and youngest member of our team, he helps out with the hardware related aspects of our robot and the assembly.

Vadász Zsolt

Role: Software Developper

Zsolt focuses on the software of the robot, both the controls, and the image recognition aspect of it. He also designed part of the circuitry, namely, our custom robotic arm control board which uses stepper motors.

Mr. Bakti András

Our mentor

Mr. András Bakti is the team’s mentor, he helps with the acquisition of needed parts, and gives advice with the robot’s assembly and programming.
